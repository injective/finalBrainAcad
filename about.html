About

Company info
Nemeth Interactive is the design studio of Adam Nemeth. Originally studied industrial design, but works mainly in the new media field, Adam is an art director / designer of websites, interactive applications and printed projects sometimes.
His studio is focusing mainly on branding, online communication and user interface design for all available platforms from mobile to web.
At work
With more than ten years background in graphic and online media design and as one of the lead designers of Walt Disney Internet Group Adam has worked with global entertainment brands like Pirates of the Caribbean, High School Musical, Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy, and the Pixar brands. He has also experience working with leading mobile operators like: T-Mobile, Vodafone, H3G, O2. From 2008 he works as the lead designer of LogMeIn Inc. the leading provider of remote acccess software solutions.
In 2007 Adam has started teaching webdesign at the KREA Design School in Budapest, where he is the leader of the webdesign and newmedia faculty from 2010.
At home
In his free time Adam is an enthusiast of photography, traditional art forms, loves cycling between the mountains, playing football or tennis with friends and pruning his bonsai trees.
